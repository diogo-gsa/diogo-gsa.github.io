Projects & Research

  1. DataStorm Project - Oct. 2013 - Sep. 2015
    DATASTORM (Large-Scale Data Management in Cloud Environments) is an initiative, lead by the Information Decision and Support Systems (IDSS) Unit of INESC-ID, for creating a critical mass of scientists and engineers for addressing the design, implementation and operation of the new wave of large-scale data-intensive software systems.

    I'm working in this project under the scope of my Master Thesis, as research fellow, and my field of action is to explore the feasibility of Data Stream Management Systems to support Energy Management applications, pointing out how to implement an Energy Management System capable of real-time data processing.

  2. DataStorm Summer School - Jul. 2014
    Took part on the organization of the 1st DataStorm Summer School, held by INESC-ID and Instituto Superior Técnico, funded by Fundação para a Ciência e Tecnologia (FCT), having been responsible for the implementation and coordination of the streaming data laboratory. [Tutorial] [EsperShell]
